about our products

Insight68 is an AI-Driven platform and set of applications driving digital transformation for manufacturers in Process and Discrete industries. Personalized Therapy has different production requirements in which it is a combination of Batch and Discrete manufacturing. Insight68 has AI-Driven capabilities to reach the goal of the "Golden Batch".

The Scalable Cloud platform delivers a fully configurable set of applications that can be deployed independently or in collaboration.

Manage all aspects of your factory or specific functional areas. The offering is designed to integrate into your current software environment.
